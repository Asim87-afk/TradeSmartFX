Forex Basics

Pips

Percentage In Point

Smallest price move. For most pairs 0.0001, JPY pairs 0.01.

Lots

Standard, Mini, Micro

Standard = 100,000 units, Mini = 10,000, Micro = 1,000

Leverage

1:10 to 1:1000

Control large positions with small capital. Double-edged sword.

Margin

Required, Used, Free

Amount needed to open and maintain positions.

Spread

Bid-Ask Difference

Difference between buy and sell price. How brokers earn.

Swap

Overnight Interest

Interest for holding positions overnight. Triple on Wednesday.

Commission

Trading Fee

Fee per trade. Usually $3-7 per lot round turn.

Slippage

Price Difference

Order executes at different price than expected.

Trading Sessions

London, NY, Tokyo

Best times: London & NY overlap (8 AM - 12 PM EST)
Indicators

EMA

Exponential Moving Average

Gives more weight to recent prices. Popular: 9, 20, 50, 200.

RSI

Relative Strength Index

0-100 scale. Above 70 overbought, below 30 oversold.

MACD

Moving Average Convergence Divergence

Shows relationship between two moving averages.

Bollinger Bands

BB

Shows volatility. Middle SMA 20, upper/lower 2 std dev.

Fibonacci

38.2%, 50%, 61.8%

Key support/resistance levels. 61.8% is golden ratio.

ATR

Average True Range

Measures volatility. Used for stop loss placement.
Candlestick Patterns

Doji

Indecision

Open and close equal. Shows market indecision.

Hammer

Bullish Reversal

Long lower wick after downtrend. Buying pressure.

Engulfing

Reversal Pattern

Second candle completely engulfs first.
Smart Money Concepts

Liquidity

Stop Loss Areas

Areas with many stop losses. Smart money hunts these.

Order Blocks

Institutional Orders

Last candle before strong move. Price returns here.

Fair Value Gap

FVG

Gap between candles from fast moves. Gets filled.

1% Rule

Risk Per Trade

Stop Loss
